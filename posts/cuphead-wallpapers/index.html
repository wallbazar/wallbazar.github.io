  1. Choose the right location. Pick a wall that is visible from most areas of the room and that doesn’t have any windows or doors.

  2. Clean the wall surface. Make sure the wall is free of dirt and dust before you start applying the wallpaper.

  3. Apply adhesive to the back of the mural wallpaper. Be generous with the adhesive so that it sticks properly to the wall surface.

  4. Hang the mural wallpaper on the wall, starting at one corner and working your way up or down. Use a smoothing tool to remove any bubbles or wrinkles.
