Tips for Choosing Stone Wallpaper: What to look for? There are a few things to look for when choosing stone wallpaper, including the type of stone, the texture, and whether or not the stone is framed. Some stones lend themselves well to being used as wallpaper because they have a smooth surface. Others, like granite, may require additional preparation such as sanding down the bumps before laying the wallpaper.
